I got invited to compete at the Conoco Phillips Innovation challenge on September 2020. The challenge was open ended and the goal was to create any algorithm which uses a machine learning algorithm to predict an event. My partner and I created an algorithm which predicted the salinity levels of a body of water. We programed our findings in a webpage alonmg with the data to support our prediction. We placed 1st and overall it was a great experience to work with a student from another university.

The company had a non-disclosure agreement on our project so I'm unable to provide the link to the website, but you are more then welcome to email me to learn more about our findings.
